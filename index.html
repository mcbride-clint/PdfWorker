PdfWorker — Features & Help

Everything runs in your browser. Your files are never uploaded anywhere.

📄
Merge & Reorder
Combine multiple PDFs. Drag pages between documents or reorder them freely.
✂
Split & Extract
Use the Split range control on any document to extract a page range as a new PDF.
🔄
Rotate Pages
Click the rotate button on a thumbnail, or right-click for the context menu.
🔍
Preview
Use the Preview button on any document to view it at full size.
🔑
Encrypted PDFs
Open password-protected PDFs — enter the password when prompted on load.
📋
Edit Metadata
Set title, author, and subject via the dialog shown before downloading.
↩
Undo / Redo
Full history in the toolbar. Keyboard: Ctrl+Z to undo, Ctrl+Y / Ctrl+Shift+Z to redo.
👆
Drag & Drop
Drop PDFs onto the upload area or directly onto a document's page grid.
📷
Multi-select
Click pages to select them; Shift+click for ranges; right-click for bulk actions.
🔒
100% Private
No uploads, no accounts, no tracking. Processing is entirely local.
